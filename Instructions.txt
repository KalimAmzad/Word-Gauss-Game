This is Word Gauss Game.

<<Run WordGaussGameDriver.py file>>
First select wordlist.txt file
Second select board.txt file
Third Enjoy the Game

<<Game Description>>
In Wordlist.txt there have some word.
You will be given a board from board.txt file

You have to gauss a word from this board either rowwise or column
wise that word is in the word list.
if your choosen word length is 3-6 : your point = word length*1
if your choosen word length is 7-9 : your point = word length*2
if your choosen word length is 10+ : your point = word length*3
